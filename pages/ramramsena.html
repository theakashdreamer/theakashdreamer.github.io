Who We Are

About Ram Ram Sena

Ram Ram Sena is a faith-driven Hindu community organization inspired by the life and teachings of Lord Ram, who symbolizes righteousness, discipline, sacrifice, and ideal leadership.

The term “Ram Ram” is a sacred greeting expressing respect and devotion, while “Sena” represents a united group working for a noble cause. Together, Ram Ram Sena stands for unity, service, and cultural preservation.

Spiritual Growth

Regular programs to strengthen faith.

Mission & Vision

To build a united, disciplined, and spiritually aware Hindu society dedicated to service and righteousness inspired by the ideals of Lord Ram.

Dharma

Upholding righteousness and moral duty in daily life.

Seva

Selfless service to humanity without expectation.

Unity

Strength in togetherness and community brotherhood.

Culture

Preserving heritage and spiritual traditions.

What We Do

Our Core Activities

Religious Programs

Ramayan Path, Sundar Kand recitations, Bhajan-Kirtan, and regular Temple seva.

Festival Celebrations

Grand celebrations of Ram Navami, Diwali, Dussehra, and Hanuman Jayanti.

Food Seva (Bhandara)

Organizing free food distribution for devotees and needy people regularly.

Blood Donation

Health awareness camps and emergency blood support initiatives.

Gau Seva

Cow protection, supporting local Gaushalas, and fodder distribution.

Social Help

Blanket drives in winter, poor family support, and disaster relief work.
